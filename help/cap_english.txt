<font size="18">Help: Capture Rules</font>

Special Cases
0x Disconnected sector.
4x Green sector with reserves.
2x Green sector without reserves.

Area modifiers
Up to +2.5x, applicable only for PvP sectors:
+0.5x for team with more territory.
+2.0x distributed based on territory ratio.

Connection modifiers
+1x Sector connection.
+1x Home base connection (for a total of 2x).
+2x Forward base connection (max reach: 2 km).

Reinforcement modifiers
+0-5x, scaled based on percent of reinforcements remaining.
If no reinforcements remain, halve all modifiers.

Capture strength
+1 infantry/crew.
+6 infantry if in stronghold (for a total of 7 points).
+0 air vehicles, UAVs.
+1 most transports.
+2 most light armed vehicles.
+3 most light APCs.
+4 most heavy APCs, light tanks.
+5 most tanks.
+6 command tanks.
+0.5 defense tiebreaker.

Requirement to capture
* Not in water.
* Not above 50m altitude.

Total capture power calculation
(1) Sum all capture strength from units.
(2) Sum all modifiers.
(3) Multiply (1) and (2).

Capture speed
Capture ratio = winning capture power / losing capture power.
Capture ratio capped at 5.0.
Capture speed: scales linearly based on capture ratio and sector size.
Fastest possible capture for size 1 sector: 90s.
Fastest possible capture for size 30 sector: 180s.
Capture decrease speed modifier: 0.75x.
